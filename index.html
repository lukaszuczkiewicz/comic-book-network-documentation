Register

Comic Book Network's account is required for using the app. The registration is 100% free.
In order to register: go to Register tab, fill username and password and click Register.
Your login and password needs to be minimum 6 and maximum 20 characters long.

How to use

Log In

Enter Username and password created during registration in order to access the dashboard.

Dashboard

Dashboard is the main view of the app. It is available after logging in.
It allows you to see the newest comics added to database. You can also read the latest comments added by the community.


  • 1 Navigation menu
  • 2 Go to your list of comics: Collected / Rated / Read / Wish List
  • 3 Newest comics added to database
  • 4 Latest comments by community

Comic Series

Click Browse Comics from menu to open the list of comic series.
Click the name of comic series to open comic series details and see all comic issues from that series.

Comic Series Details

This view shows information about the series of comic books and include the list of every added comic issue.
Click the comic card to open comic details. You can also add comics to your lists: collection/read/wish list.

Comic Details

This view shows detail information about the particular comic issue.
Click star buttons to rate the comic (min=1 star, max=5 stars). You can delete rating by clicking X button. Rating comics automatically adds the comic to your "read" list.
You can also add comics to your lists: collection/read/wish list. Adding to collection automatically removes the comic from your wish list. Adding to wish list automatically removes the comic from your collection.
Under the comic detials there is a comment section. You can preview comments from all users, add and delete your own comments.

Edit profile

This view allows to preview and edit your profile data: country and description (about me).
